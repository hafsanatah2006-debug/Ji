FOUNDER'S JOURNEY

Hafsa's Struggle & Triumph

Hafsa faced endless job rejections, financial hardship, and the pain of losing her stable corporate role during uncertain times. With a dream buried beneath doubt, she started baking at 3 AM, borrowed a small espresso machine, and sold coffee at local pop-ups. Despite countless closed doors, her unwavering passion gave birth to Caffeine Cove — a sanctuary for dreamers. Today, she stands as a beacon of resilience, employing single mothers and artists. Her story reminds us: the strongest brews emerge from the darkest roasts. ☕✨

"Every rejection brewed a stronger version of me. Now we serve hope in a cup." – Hafsa
